    About

STRAIN is an upcoming story-driven survival game developed and published by Lux Games.

Set in a neon apocalypse, the game challenges players to survive within city walls by scavenging for resources, crafting, cooking, building and fending off other scavengers.

Players can join factions, each offering unique benefits and altering the course of the story based on their choices.
