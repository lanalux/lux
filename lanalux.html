    About

Lana Lux is an independent game developer, designer, and Twitch streamer based in Toronto. She is known for her work on games such as "Don't Notice Me" and is currently working on "STRAIN," an apocalyptic survival game set in Toronto.

Lana Lux is actively involved in creating an accessible and supportive community for aspiring game developers. She uses her platform on Twitch to educate and inspire new voices in game development, sharing her process and facilitating conversations around game development.
